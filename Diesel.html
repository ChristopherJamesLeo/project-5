Diesel

Fueling Industries, Moving Economies

We started our diesel business in August 2015.


We sell and distribute diesel to retail customers at our 24-hour fuel station, which operates under the brand “Cnergy” at 50 Old Toh Tuck Road.


The station, which is open to the public, has 5 diesel dispensers with 4 nozzles each and two 20,000-litre underground diesel storage tanks which houses our diesel supply.


We also transport, distribute and bulk sell diesel to commercial customers.

Drive Smoother, Drive Further

Formulated to deliver superior fuel economy and a smoother drive.

An established provider of fuel products in Singapore with over 40 years of operating track record in the business of Retail LPG, CNG and Diesel.
